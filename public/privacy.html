Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

1. Introduction

AI Custom Bot ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle the data you provide for your custom chatbot and the data collected from your website visitors.

2. Data We Collect

  • Client Data: Your name, email, website URL, and the content you provide (PDFs, text) for training the bot.
  • Chat Logs: The conversations that occur between the AI and your website visitors.
  • Leads: Contact information (name, email) captured by the bot from your visitors.

3. Data Usage & Protection

We do not sell your data. We use your website content and documents solely to train and improve your specific AI chatbot instance. We do not use your proprietary data to train models for other clients.

4. Third-Party Processors

To deliver this service, we transmit data to trusted third-party infrastructure providers, including:

  • AI Models: (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic) for generating responses.
  • Vector Databases: For storing your training content securely.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: (e.g., Vercel, Cloudflare) for hosting.

5. Your Rights

You may request a copy of the data we hold about you or request the deletion of your training data at any time by emailing hello@aicustombot.net.
